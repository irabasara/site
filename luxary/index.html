OUR STORY / HERITAGE

Crafted where the legends were born.

Origins

ARAME is more than just brandy; it's a journey through time, a testament to Armenian heritage, and a celebration of craftsmanship. Our story begins in the heart of Armenia, where the ancient traditions of winemaking and distillation have been passed down through generations.



Nestled in the fertile valleys of Armenia, our vineyards benefit from the unique terroir, characterized by mineral-rich soils and a continental climate. This exceptional environment imparts distinctive flavors to our grapes, the foundation of ARAME's exceptional character.

Master of Distillers

Our master distillers are the guardians of tradition, blending time-honored techniques with modern innovation. With decades of experience, they meticulously oversee every step of the process, from grape selection to distillation and aging, ensuring that each bottle of ARAME embodies the essence of Armenian brandy-making.

Serving

To fully appreciate the rich flavors of ARAME, serve it in a tulip-shaped glass at room temperature. This allows the aromas to develop and the complex notes to be savored. Pour a small amount, swirl gently, and inhale the bouquet before taking a sip. ARAME pairs beautifully with a variety of foods. Its robust character complements dark chocolate, aged cheeses, and grilled meats. For a lighter pairing, consider dried fruits or nuts. Experiment to find your perfect match.

Enhance your ARAME experience with carefully curated moments. Pair it with a fine cigar and your favorite jazz record for a relaxing evening. Or, enjoy it with a decadent dessert like a chocolate lava cake to elevate your dining experience.
